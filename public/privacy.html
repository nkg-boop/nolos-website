Privacy policy

Last updated: 30 June 2026

Draft notice: the technical measures described here (encryption, access control, cookie behaviour) are implemented in the site's code as described. The legal wording itself is still a starting template, not finished legal advice — have it reviewed by someone qualified in South African data protection law (POPIA) before publishing, especially given that enquiries may include details about children.

What we collect

When you submit our contact form, we collect the name, phone number, and optionally the email address you provide, along with which services you're enquiring about and any notes you choose to share — which may include your children's ages or care needs.

How we use it

We use this information only to respond to your enquiry, including by following up on WhatsApp or by phone, and to keep a record of placement enquiries for our own business purposes. We do not sell or share your information with third parties for marketing.

Where it's stored

Enquiries submitted through this site are stored on our server, encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM, an industry-standard encryption method. This protects the data if the underlying server storage is ever accessed without authorization. Encryption at rest is one layer of protection, not the only one — access to view enquiries also requires a separate authenticated login limited to Nolos staff.

Cookies

This website does not use cookies for advertising, tracking, or analytics, and browsing the public pages of this site — home, about, services, testimonials, and contact — does not set any cookie on your device.

The only cookie this site ever sets is a single session cookie, and it is only created when a Nolos staff member logs into the password-protected admin area to view submitted enquiries. That cookie:

  • is not accessible to JavaScript (marked HttpOnly), which protects it from certain types of attack;
  • is only ever sent over an encrypted HTTPS connection once the site is live (marked Secure);
  • is never sent to any other website (marked SameSite=Strict);
  • automatically expires after one hour of login; and
  • contains no personal information about site visitors — only a reference to a staff login session.

If you are a parent filling out our contact form, you will never receive this cookie. Because this site sets no cookies for ordinary visitors, no cookie consent banner is shown — one would have nothing to ask consent for.

Data retention and security

We keep enquiry records for as long as reasonably needed to respond to your enquiry and for our own business record-keeping, and delete or anonymize records on request (see "Your rights" below). Access to stored enquiries is restricted to authorised Nolos staff through a password-protected login, and the underlying data is encrypted at rest as described above.

Children's information

Because our service involves childcare, enquiries may include information about minors. We treat this information with the same care we expect from our nannies, and access to stored enquiries should be limited to authorised Nolos staff only.

Your rights

Under POPIA, you can ask us what information we hold about you, request corrections, or ask us to delete it. Contact us using the details below to make a request.
