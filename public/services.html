Services

Care that fits your family's rhythm

Every Nolos nanny completes the same vetting trail, no matter which service you choose. Pick what fits, mix and match as your schedule changes.

Stay-in nanny placement

A nanny lives in your home, usually Monday to Saturday, so your children have round-the-clock care and continuity. No daily handover, no commute to worry about.

Day nanny placement

A nanny comes to your home Monday to Friday, 8am-5pm, and goes home after her shift. In-home care for the full working day, without a live-in arrangement.

After-school nanny placement

Your nanny collects the kids from school, helps with homework, prepares food, and stays until you're home from work. Ideal for families who only need coverage for part of the day.

Sleepover & weekend care

Overnight or weekend care for late work commitments, travel, or events. The same vetted nanny your children already know, available when plans run past bedtime.

Public holiday cover

Coverage for school holidays and public holidays, when your usual routine doesn't apply but your work schedule still does.
