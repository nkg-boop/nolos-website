About Nolos Nanny Placement

Tembisa parents, building trust in childcare

We are Nolos Nanny Placement, a division of Nolos Safe Kids Care (Pty) Ltd. We're a BBBEE compliant, SARS registered nanny agency based in Tembisa, serving all of Gauteng. Our purpose is to make hiring a nanny safe, simple, and stress-free for parents across the province.

Why we started Nolos

Every family on our books has the same worry at the start: can I really trust this person in my home, with my children? We built Nolos around answering that question properly, not just with reassurance, but with a process — ID checks, reference calls, and SAPS clearance — before a nanny ever meets your family.

We're registered with the CIPC (Reg. 9461359383), BBBEE compliant, and SARS registered, based right here in Tembisa, Zone 8. We're not a faceless app. We're parents and neighbours who happen to run a placement agency.

What we stand for

Safety first, always — no nanny is placed without completing our full vetting trail.

Real relationships — we stay in touch with families after placement, not just before.

Rooted in Gauteng — we know the communities we place nannies into.

Our vetting trail

How every Nolos nanny earns your trust

ID verified

We confirm identity documents against official records before any further screening begins.

References checked

We speak directly with past families and employers — not just read what's written down.

SAPS clearance

A police clearance certificate is on file before a nanny is ever introduced to a family.

Have questions about our process? Get in touch — we're happy to walk you through it.
